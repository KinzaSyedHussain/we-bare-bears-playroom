# We Bare Bears playroom 🐻

This is a simple, casual digital "quiet book" project I built to play around with different We Bare Bears scenes and character stickers. 
You can swap out backgrounds from the show, drag Grizz, Panda, and Ice Bear into the room, and set up your own scenes.

* **Endless Bears:** You can drag as many copies of the bears as you want from the sidebar onto the screen.
* **Drop Anywhere:** The tracking logic calculates the exact coordinates where you release your mouse, so you can place characters cleanly on the bed, floor, or lawn.
* **Quick Clean Up:** If you change your mind, just double-click any single bear to delete it, or hit the red "Clear All" button to clean up the entire board at once.
* **4 Scenes:** Switch between the Bedroom, Hallway, Outside the Cave, and the Forest.
